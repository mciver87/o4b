Moe & D’s Restaurant Grill & Bar

Moe & D’s Restaurant Grill & Bar is a local family-owned business established by Steve, Michael, and Moe Deloach, located at downtown Rocky Mount at 123 South Church Street, Rocky Mount, North Carolina. Moe & D’s is open for lunch and early evening dinner, offering a variety of Southern soul foods, as well as featuring a full service bar. The business is an advocate for downtown and is quickly establishing itself as a gathering and uplifting space for all of our Rocky Mount community.
